# 在三層環境中佈建 192 GB 及 32 GB 伺服器
{: #install-256GB}

| --- | --- |

|2 |[準備伺服器進行 SAP 安裝](/docs/infrastructure/sap-netweaver-ms-qrg/ms-prepare-server-256GB.html) |
|3 |[分割及檔案系統](/docs/infrastructure/sap-netweaver-ms-qrg/ms-partition-256GB.html) |
